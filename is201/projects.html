Experience Abroad

Australia

At age 7 my father got a promotion at his job that moved us overseas to Sydney, Australia for nearly 5 years. While there, we experienced foreign customs, food, slang, and incredible diversity. We learned about Indigenous culture, multiculturalism, and Australian values. They are very passionate about their sports, music, food, and environmentalism.



Barcelona

While in highschool I traveled with my mother to visit Barcelona for a week. We enjoyed the rich culture, delicious food and unique architecture - such as Antoni Gaudi's "La Sagrada Familia." We also got to see vibrant celebrations of Catalan traditions and even an FC Barcelona game.

Ecuador

After graduation I left for a month to Cuenca, Ecuador on a humanitarian trip. We helped build new housing at a center for those with special needs. On the weekends we got to immerse ourselves in the culture, visiting waterfalls, museums, tiendas, and even spent a couple days at the Galapogos Islands.



Thailand

While living in Australia, we were able to visit a few nearby countries, including Thailand. Our trip coincided with their nation's water celebration known as "Songkran." Locals and tourists alike engage in joyful water fights to cleanse the past year's misfortunes and usher in the new year with good wishes. It was particularly an eye-opening experience to learn about their passionate religious practices and overall cheerful natures.



Latin America

This year I was fortunate enough to be a part of BYU's Latin America Business Study Abroad program over spring semester. We learned from and interacted with locals in cities across Brazil, Argentina, Uruguay, Paraguay and Costa Rica.
